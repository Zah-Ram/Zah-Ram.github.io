I am a PhD candidate specializing in Medical Artificial Intelligence, working under the supervision of Professor Kim Jin Young. My research focuses on developing intelligent systems that can assist clinicians in diagnostic decision-making.

My work spans uncertainty quantification, contrastive learning, and vision-language models for radiology applications. I'm particularly interested in making AI systems more reliable and interpretable for clinical deployment.
